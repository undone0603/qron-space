QronCode Stack

Four layers of technology, one seamless verification experience. Each layer builds on the last to deliver unbreakable provenance.

Layer 1: Visual QR

Artistic QR generation with brand customization

Each QronCode is a unique piece of generative art — combining brand colors, patterns, and gradients into a scannable visual identity. No two QronCodes are alike. Machine-readable, human-beautiful.

Layer 2: AI StoryMode

AI-powered narrative attached to each scan

Every scan triggers a rich, AI-generated story about the product's journey — from origin to shelf. StoryMode transforms cold verification data into compelling brand narratives that consumers actually want to read.

Layer 3: Blockchain Anchor

Immutable provenance record on Polygon

Every QronCode seal is anchored to the Polygon blockchain — creating an immutable, timestamped provenance record. No central authority can alter or revoke a seal once minted. Truth, permanently recorded.

Layer 4: AutoFlow

Make.com automation for minting, verification, alerts

AutoFlow connects minting, scanning, and alerting through intelligent automation. When a product is sealed, verified, or flagged — the protocol responds instantly. Zero manual intervention. Full pipeline automation.

Architecture

Built on Solidity. Secured by Design.

Six modular smart contracts power the QRON protocol. Each one auditable, upgradeable, and purpose-built.

QRONToken.sol

ERC-20 core with burn mechanics and governance hooks

The foundational token contract implementing ERC-20 with built-in burn-on-transfer mechanics. Includes governance weight calculation hooks, allowance management, and compatibility with staking vault deposits.

ProtocolFeeRouter.sol

Routes fees: 20% Burn, 30% Security, 30% AI Compute, 20% Treasury

Deterministic fee routing contract that splits every protocol fee into four buckets. Immutable allocation ratios enforced on-chain. Includes emergency pause and admin override for security events.

RewardsBudgetContract.sol

Revenue-funded emission scheduler with epoch controls

Emissions are never inflationary — they're budgeted from protocol revenue each epoch. The scheduler calculates reward pools based on real income, distributing proportionally across staking tiers.

FiatSwapBridge.sol

USD → $QRON conversion layer for brand onboarding

The bridge contract that abstracts crypto complexity from brand clients. USD payments trigger automated market buys of $QRON, routing tokens directly to fee coverage. Oracle-priced, slippage-protected.

StakingVault.sol

Tiered staking with lockup periods and multiplier rewards

Four-tier staking vault with time-locked deposits. Longer lockups earn higher multipliers. Integrates with governance for voting weight calculation. Auto-compounding optional per user preference.

CampaignPool.sol

Brand campaign funding and reward distribution engine

Brands fund verification campaigns that reward consumers for scanning and engaging with QronCodes. Supports time-bounded campaigns, reward caps, and multi-brand co-funding arrangements.
